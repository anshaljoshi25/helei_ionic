Help 1) What is this app about?

Helei is for you! Find out what's happening around you, go hangout there, share information about parties, conferences, sports meetups and many more such types of events!

2) What is an event?

An event is literally anything that happens. We think that events are, but not limited to; specials, deals, advertisements, gatherings, fundraisers, conferences, etc. If you have something that you want to post to the public visit us at http://www.heleiapp.co/#!venues/a1fad for more information. Or contact us at 815 566 9552 or email us at info@heleiapp.co.

3) How do I post public events?

Venue owners can visit our website http://www.heleiapp.co/ [venue owners section] to get started. Or contact us at 815 566 9552 or email us at info@heleiapp.co for help and guidance.

4) What are private events?

A private event is anything that you want to inform your group of friends about. This event will not be shown to others than the people you invite. If you want to privately invite your friends to an event you can do so by sharing the event to your friends directly.
